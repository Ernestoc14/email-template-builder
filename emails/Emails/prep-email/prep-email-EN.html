4. Information for Domestic Flights

Direct flights to David

Go to Terminal 2 on Tocumen Airport, to the departures area (Level 2), in front of the entrance P19 you will find the exclusive counters for domestic flights. Then, walk down the hallway that will take you to the boarding lounge and the security checkpoint.

Connecting flights to David

When disembarking in Panama, go to Immigration and Customs in Terminal 2. When exiting, go up to Level 2 and walk to the exclusive counters for domestic flights, in front of the entrance P19, and follow the signs to the boarding lounge. Remember that your baggage will be checked until David.
